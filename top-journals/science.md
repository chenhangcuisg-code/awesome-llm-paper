# LLM Papers — Science Family (AAAS)

Verified large-language-model–related papers from journals in the **Science** family (Science, Science Advances, Science Robotics), covering 2023–2026.

**Source verification.** Each entry has a confirmed DOI verified via web search snippets (science.org pages block direct WebFetch, so verification relies on multiple corroborating sources including PubMed, ADS, and publisher pages). Entries 24–31 are 2026 publications based on search-result metadata; exact issue/month should be reconfirmed at point of citation.

**Coverage scope.** Protein/genomic foundation models, LLM-for-medicine, autonomous lab/agent systems, robotics foundation models (VLA), policy/perspective pieces. Related papers from *Nature* (Coscientist, Med-PaLM, ESM-3 perspective, AMIE) are catalogued in [`nature.md`](./nature.md) instead.
