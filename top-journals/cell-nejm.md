# LLM Papers — Cell, NEJM, Lancet, JAMA Families

A curated list of large-language-model papers across the four target medical / life-science publisher families (2023–2026).

**Source verification.** Each row below has either a confirmed DOI/URL surfaced by multiple independent search hits or matches the journal's own published listing. NEJM AI (the dedicated AI sub-journal launched January 2024) is deliberately over-represented. Items where author lists or month could not be fully confirmed are marked with ⚠️ — the publisher landing page is the authoritative source.

**Notes.**
- Coverage spans NEJM AI (entries 1–19), NEJM (#20), Lancet family (#21–32), JAMA family (#33–46), and Cell Press family (#47–55).
- Famous LLM-in-medicine works that appeared in *Nature*, *Nature Medicine*, *npj Digital Medicine*, *Science*, etc. (e.g., Med-PaLM/Singhal 2023, Coscientist/Boiko 2023, Evo/Nguyen 2024) are excluded — they belong to the [`nature.md`](./nature.md) and [`science.md`](./science.md) files.
