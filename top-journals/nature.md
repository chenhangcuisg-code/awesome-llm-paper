## Categories with no journal-only hits (intentional gaps)

- **Anthropic-authored papers in Nature/Science**: Anthropic publishes via arXiv + Transformer Circuits Thread. The only mainstream coverage is the Nature *News & Views* on Sleeper Agents (#72 above).
- **Mechanistic interpretability / SAE / dictionary learning** as primary research articles in Nature/Science: still arXiv-only.
- **Inverse scaling / broken scaling laws**: Caballero (broken neural scaling), McKenzie (Inverse Scaling Prize) remain arXiv / ICLR — the closest journal hit is the "Densing law" (#73 above).
- **WebArena / GAIA / SWE-bench**: benchmarks themselves remain conference / arXiv venues, not Nature/Science.
- **RLHF / RLAIF / DPO / InstructGPT / GRPO** in Nature/Science journals: all arXiv / NeurIPS only. The only Nature peer-reviewed RL-LLM paper is DeepSeek-R1 (#62) and the "narrow-task broad misalignment" finetuning paper (#145).
- **Toolformer / SayCan / PaLM-E / RT-2 / OpenVLA / π0 / ReAct**: all blog / arXiv / conference venues, no Nature/Science peer review.
- **MuZero / AlphaGo / AlphaGo Zero / AlphaZero / AlphaDev**: published in Nature/Science but their architectures are CNN / ResNet + MCTS, **not** transformer foundation models — intentionally excluded from this transformer-foundation list (though Nature/Science papers).
- **AlphaProteo, AlphaFold-Multimer**: remain DeepMind blog / bioRxiv preprints, no Nature/Science version found.
- **ProtTrans, ProtBERT, ProtT5, ESM-IF1, Tranception, SaProt, Boltz-1, Uni-Mol, ChemBERTa, CellPLM, tGPT, BioBERT, PubMedBERT, SciBERT**: all at ML venues (TPAMI, ICLR, NeurIPS, ICML) or *Bioinformatics*, not in the Nature / Science / Cell / NEJM ecosystem.
