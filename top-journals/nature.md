# LLM Papers — Nature Family

A curated list of large-language-model–related papers across the **Nature portfolio** (Springer Nature) — covering Nature, Nature Medicine, Nature Machine Intelligence, Nature Methods, Nature Biotechnology, Nature Biomedical Engineering, Nature Computational Science, Nature Communications, Nature Human Behaviour, Nature Reviews series, and npj Digital Medicine. Period: 2022–2026.

**Source verification.** All DOIs and links below were verified via search-engine returns of nature.com canonical URLs. Full-text WebFetch is blocked by Nature's IDP authentication redirect, so titles/authors/years rely on multi-source corroboration in search snippets. Author lists labeled "Authors per landing page" indicate partial uncertainty about full ordering — the DOI/URL is authoritative.
